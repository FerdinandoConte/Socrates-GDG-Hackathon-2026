Our approach

  • ✓Question-first, by design

    A guardrail intercepts shortcut prompts and replies with a constructive refusal.

  • ✓Adaptive pacing

    Real-time delta scoring drives the difficulty curve. Hints when stuck, acceleration when flying.

  • ✓Rubric-graded mastery

    Each session ends with a verifiable map of what you know — and what to revisit.

  • ✓Active production

    Mind-maps, voice explanations, Feynman tests. The learner produces; SOCRATES questions.

The method

Three agents. One outcome.

01

The Hook

The Curious Agent opens with metaphors, real-world cases and a high-level mind map — never a flat summary.

Curious Agent
02

The Dialogue

The Socratic Agent rejects passive prompts and drives you toward your cognitive limits.

Stuck? Skip a question — the agent learns and recalibrates.

Socratic Agent

The Verdict

The output: a transparent map of your real mastery — and the next step.

SOCRATES is benchmarked along two axes: pedagogical quality (rubric-graded relevance and Socratic adherence) and learner outcome (post-session recall, transfer, Feynman scores). Every interaction is a closed Q → A → F → R loop: question posed, answer given, feedback computed from the delta, next question refined. Pacing reacts in real time to evasiveness, skips and audio-hesitancy markers.
